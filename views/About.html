About Kallamino Special High School

This Special High school is only dedicated to Tigrian brilliant
students located on Mekelle it was established since 1990 E.c (19998 GC). This school was one of
the expression of tigray's students excellency in acadamic and ethics. the main purpose is to
eliminate poverty and lack of educated man in this region with respective economical status.
it mainly focuses on Natural science and Ethics with Technology.
